Our Story

DoraCatering, established in 2015 in the UK, offers exceptional Romanian Catering Services for private events, including weddings, christenings, and birthday parties.

We provide both standard and personalized menus of the highest quality to make every occasion unforgettable!

What we can offer

We take pride in offering a diverse and authentic selection of Romanian dishes, crafted with passion and tradition. Whether you are looking for a traditional feast or a modern twist on classic recipes, our menu has something for everyone.

Meet Our Chefs

Corneliu Spânu, the esteemed owner and founder of Dora Catering (established in 2015), is a highly skilled Romanian chef with a rich culinary journey spanning over two decades. His career showcases extensive experience in both Romania and the UK, where he has mastered a variety of cuisines and developed an exceptional reputation for quality and creativity.

Romanian Experience:

  • Restaurant Pinocchio (Iași) – Chef (2005)
  • Restaurant Bradet (Suceava) – Sous Chef (2007)
  • Restaurant Capriciu (Suceava) – Head Chef (2008)

UK Experience:

  • The Barley Mow (Abingdon) – Chef (2010), Head Chef (2011)
  • Byron (Oxford) – Head Chef (2012)
  • Crown Plazza (Chipping Norton) – Private Chef (2013)

Nicoleta Spânu, The second owner is a passionate chef who inherited her love for cooking from her father. Together with her husband, she deepened her culinary knowledge and started organizing private events in Romania.

UK Experience:

  • The Barley Mow (Abingdon) – Chef (2011)
  • Byron (Oxford) – Second Chef (2012 - 2022)
