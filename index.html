Meet Our Chef

Corneliu Spanu, the esteemed owner and founder of Dora Catering (established in 2015), is a highly skilled Romanian chef with a rich culinary journey spanning over two decades. His career showcases extensive experience in both Romania and the UK, where he has mastered a variety of cuisines and developed an exceptional reputation for quality and creativity.

Romanian Experience:

  • Restaurant Pinocchio (Iași) – Chef (2005)
  • Restaurant Bradet (Suceava) – Sous Chef (2007)
  • Restaurant Capriciu (Suceava) – Head Chef (2008)

UK Experience:

  • The Barley Mow (Abingdon) – Chef (2010), Head Chef (2011)
  • Byron (Oxford) – Head Chef (2012)
  • Crown Plazza (Chipping Norton) – Private Chef (2013)
